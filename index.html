About Us

It all started with three friends, Osama, Bahaa, and Mohammad, who shared a love for food but struggled to cook anything edible. After countless failed attempts in the kitchen, they decided to turn their mishaps into a mission.

CookLab was born out of their determination to make cooking accessible and fun for everyone, so no one has to endure the disasters they did. Join us as we turn failures into flavorful successes!
